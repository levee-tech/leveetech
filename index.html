Bull Case

$12.3B+ in announced hyperscale capex creates structural demand through 2027+. Certified industrial park with limited supply offers build-to-suit optionality at $40-60K/acre.

⚠️

Key Risk Uncovered

Multiple announcements may overlap — true incremental investment could be lower than headline numbers suggest, shortening the construction demand runway.

🎯

Recommendation

Buy at 7%+ cap rate, plan for a 3-year hold with re-tenanting at market. Skip if 10-year stability is required — this is opportunistic infrastructure-adjacent flex.

Hidden Factor

Former biotech lab may require specialized tenanting or environmental remediation, narrowing the buyer pool beyond standard flex — not visible in typical market data.

This is a bet on hyperscale AI infrastructure build-out continuing through 2027+. The NNN lease provides clean cash flow during the hold. At the right price, it's a solid opportunistic play for an investor who can manage turnover.
